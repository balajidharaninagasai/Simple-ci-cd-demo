My Favourite Places

1. Kerala

Kerala, also known as "God's Own Country," offers a diverse range of attractions for tourists

2. TajMahal

The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, on the banks of the Yamuna River. It is widely known as a symbol of love and is one of the most famous mausoleums in the world

3. Lambasingi

Lambasingi (or Lammasingi) is a small village in the Eastern Ghats of Chintapalli Mandal of Alluri Sitharama Raju district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It's also referred as "The Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh".
